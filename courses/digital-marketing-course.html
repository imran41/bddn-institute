📣 Course Overview

The BDDN Digital Marketing Specialist program provides a comprehensive roadmap to mastering the modern online advertising landscape. Standard marketing strategies are evolving fast, and businesses are shifting heavily towards performance-driven digital ads. You will learn the core logic of search engine optimization, pay-per-click management, visual content planning, and conversion tracking.

We believe in a practical-first approach. You will create, target, and launch mock advertising campaigns, audit live websites for Google SEO, and set up analytics pipelines to track marketing ROI.

🎯 What You Will Learn

  • ✔ Search Engine Optimization (SEO): Rank keywords on Google Page 1.
  • ✔ Google Search & Display Ads: Manage paid search and bidding campaigns.
  • ✔ Meta Ads Manager: Budget and target Instagram & Facebook advertising.
  • ✔ Google Analytics (GA4): Measure funnel conversion and site traffic.
  • ✔ Lead Generation: Design landing pages and opt-in automation pipelines.
